Power Market

The biggest challenge to overcome while designing for Power Market, was pertaining to data visualization. Essentially: how to turn satellite data on solar energy KPIs into an intuitive SaaS platform? I worked with the founders designing and developing their product from scratch, building their prototype, and designing their deck and pitch.

UrbanSDK

With UrbanSDK, we wanted to demonstrate that with the advent of COVID-19, an absence of early, accurate information from governments set off a butterfly effect and we missed the opportunity to be proactive. Enter: Urban SDK; a platform to change that paradigm. On a mission to fix what went wrong and use the power of data automation and machine learning to empower proactive leadership in cities around the world - by helping governments in four key areas: streamlining data collection; automating analysis and reports; predicting needs and impacts; and integrating all systems and data collection devices.

GetHenry

The design challenge was to promote micro-mobility from a different angle. By integrating two-wheeled light electric vehicles into corporate environments such as hotels, business campuses, real estate or last-mile delivery, they manage to eliminate the majority of problems micro-mobility is facing today while adding real value to businesses.

V2X Network

The big design thinking challenge with V2X, was to find a way to effectively communicate what they do, without saying that it's 'an autonomous vehicle, fintech network built on blockchain;' and to support that script with aesthetically appealing, supportive slides.
